about

So a little about myself... I am currently enrolled in the University of Richmond’s Coding Boot Camp (graduation in October), where I have had the pleasure to work with great staff and fellow students in developing the skills necessary to build full-stack web applications.

During my time in the boot camp, I have developed/co-developed over 10 applications. These include everything from basic single-page static websites to full-stack multi-page applications. I have also had the opportunity to work with classmates on original projects, which were challenging but great learning experiences that taught me the importance of both teamwork and autonomy.

Perhaps most importantly, I have a passion for coding and a drive to see projects through. I am devoted to learning anything and everything I can, whether it’s the fundamentals or the latest and greatest library/framework.
